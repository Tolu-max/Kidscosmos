Conjunction

Introduction to conjunction

  • Written by KidsCosmos Team

Study carefully the words printed in bold type below

He walked quickly along the jungle track and felt very happy, till he met a lion.

The loin could not stop because it was running too fast.

TRhe words and, till and because are used to join sentences. Such words are called conjunctions. Cconjuctions join sentences or words.

Study the following examples:

  • Peter and Paul came here.
  • He played untill the rain stopped.
  • I was reading when he arrived.
  • She said that her book was lost.
  • Either Abu or Eze sang yesterday.
  • He failed the test because he was careless.
  • They want to the market but did not buy anything.
  • She punished the boy for she was annoyed with him.
  • I knocked before I opened the door.
  • Be careful lest you fall.
  • Yyou must think as you write.

Other conjuctions in common use are:
if, which, although, since, that, whether, except, unless, so, so that,etc.
