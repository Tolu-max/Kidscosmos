Pronoun Basics

  • written by KidsCosmos Team

Pronouns are substitutes for nouns.

Examine the paragraph below:
I am Kate, and my brother's name is Ken. He is a footballer, and he plays for our village. The villagers love my brother because he is a dedicated player. They sponsored him to travel abroad to play for our town. As a gift for my brother, we bought a briefcase for him. It would be useful in keeping vital information.

Look at the three words printed in bold type in the passage above. He is used in place of Ken. They stands for the villagers. We stands for everyone. It stands for the briefcase. A word used instead of nouns is called a Pronoun.

# Singular Plural
1 I We
2 Me Us
3 Mine Ours
4 Myself Ourselves
5 You You
6 Yours Yours
7 Yourself Yourselves
8 She They
9 Her Them
10 Hers Theirs
"You should try to learn as much as you can and also understand it."

  1. The Relative Pronouns points to, or is related to a noun or pronoun going before. The words below are Relative Pronouns:

    Who What
    Whom Which
    Whose That

  2. Other Pronouns are:

    Either Neither
    Any All
    Some Many

CORRECT USAGE OF PRONOUNS

Study the following sentences and note the common errors:

# Incorrect Correct
1 John is taller than me. John is taller than I (am).
2 It seems to be her. It seems to be she.
3 Mark is as fat as them. Mark is as fat as they (are).
4 Him and me passed the test. He and I passed the test.
5 The ant entered it's hole. The ant entered its hole.
6 It is me. It is I.
7 Divide the apple between he and she. Divide the apple between him and her.
8 We supposed it was him. We supposed it was he.
9 She is as short as him. She is as short as he (is).
10 Let you and I do it. Let you and me do it.
11 Who did you give the pen? To whom did you give the pen?
12 Either him or her can swim. Either he or she can swim.
13 Wait for she and I. Wait for her and me.
14 Whom are you? Who are you?
15 They are stronger than us. They are stronger than we (are).
16 It was him who did it. It was he who did it.
17 Paul and me saw the dog. Paul and I saw the dog.
18 It is us who you saw. It is we whom you saw.
